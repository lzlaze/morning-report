## 2. ES vs YM DIVERGENCE ANALYSIS

YM is underperforming ES by ~19bps (-1.04% vs -0.85%), which at first glance seems counterintuitive given that today's sector leadership shows Financials (+1.21%) and Industrials (+0.63%) — both Dow-heavy sectors — leading. The likely explanation: the crude oil spike is hammering transportation and manufacturing-cost-sensitive Dow components (think CAT, BA, HON, UNH with healthcare cost sensitivity), and the price-weighted nature of YM means a few high-dollar names dragging hard can overwhelm the sector tailwinds. Meanwhile, ES is getting a slight relative cushion from the defensive tilt in its broader diversification. The real underperformer is NQ (-0.95%), suggesting mega-cap tech is under pressure too — this is a broad de-risking event, not a rotation. Watch the energy-driven names in the Dow specifically (CVX is a Dow component and crude is ripping — that should be a partial offset, so something else in the Dow is bleeding harder).
