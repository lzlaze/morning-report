## 2. ES vs YM DIVERGENCE ANALYSIS

Notable divergence: ES -0.95% vs YM -0.56%. That's a ~40bps gap, which is meaningful in pre-market.

The explanation is straightforward: this is a tech problem, not a broad market problem. NQ at -1.50% tells you mega-cap tech is getting hit hard. S&P carries ~30% tech weight, so ES inherits that pain directly. The Dow, weighted toward industrials, financials, and healthcare with far less pure tech exposure (MSFT and AAPL are in there, but diluted across 30 names on price-weighting), is cushioned. The sector leadership data confirms it — Financials +1.21% and Industrials +0.63% are the two pillars of the Dow, and they're green. That's your divergence engine.

Russell only down 40bps reinforces this: small caps don't care about mega-cap tech multiple compression. If you're long YM vs short ES, this morning is working for you. Watch whether this divergence widens or compresses at the open — if financials/industrials roll over, YM catches down fast.
