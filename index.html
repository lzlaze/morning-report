## 2. ES vs YM DIVERGENCE ANALYSIS

YM is underperforming ES by ~19bps (-0.99% vs -0.80%). This makes sense given the catalyst. The Dow is heavier in industrials (CAT, HON, BA), financials (GS, JPM), and consumer names (WMT, HD, MCD) — all of which get squeezed directly by surging energy input costs and the margin compression that implies. ES is holding up marginally better because Energy is the top sector at +2.48% and tech (+0.48%) is catching a relative bid — both are larger weights in the S&P than in the Dow. Chevron is the only meaningful energy name in the DJIA, so the Dow gets less of the energy offset. This is a classic oil-shock divergence pattern — expect YM to continue lagging if crude stays bid.
