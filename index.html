## 2. ES vs YM DIVERGENCE ANALYSIS

Clear divergence. YM underperforming ES by ~54 bps. This is textbook for an oil shock session. The Dow is heavy with industrials (Boeing, Caterpillar, 3M), financials (Goldman, JPM), and consumer-facing names (McDonald's, Walmart, Home Depot) — all of which get hit directly by rising input costs and margin compression from an energy spike. ES is cushioned because NQ is essentially flat (+0.01%), and mega-cap tech (~30% of SPX weight) is largely energy-agnostic on the cost side and even benefits as a relative haven in stagflation rotations. The Russell (-0.36%) confirms this is hitting the real economy more than large-cap tech. If crude holds above $86, expect YM to continue lagging ES through the session.
