## 2. ES vs YM DIVERGENCE ANALYSIS

YM is underperforming ES by roughly 40bps — notable divergence. The Dow's heavier weighting toward industrials (CAT, HON, BA), financials (GS, JPM), and consumer-facing names (WMT, HD, MCD) makes it more vulnerable to the crude oil spike, which pressures input costs and squeezes margins for capital-intensive and consumer-discretionary-adjacent businesses. Meanwhile, ES is being cushioned by tech, which is the clear leader today at +0.44% — mega-cap tech (AAPL, MSFT, NVDA, META) comprising ~30% of SPX is acting as a relative safe haven and absorbing flows. NQ barely red at -0.05% confirms this. The divergence is oil-driven: YM's composition eats the cost, SPX's tech weight absorbs the bid. If you're spreading ES/YM, the compression trade only works if oil reverses — otherwise YM stays heavy.
